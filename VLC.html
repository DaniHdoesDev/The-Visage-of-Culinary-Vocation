About the Chef

Verneal was born and raised up in Chicago, IL. She has made a career as a passionate home chef. She has dedicated her culinary craft to ignite the sense and keep traditions alive. Many of her recipes are derived from helping her mother in the kitchen and other black female chefs. Through experimenting her afrocentric approach to gourmet cuisines, her flavor profiles are unlike any other.
